Importing Bitcoins in Coinbase

After creating your Coinbase account, go to Account Settings > Integrations, and import a paper wallet. Take a picture of the QR code on your tip sheet, or type it in manually.

Importing Bitcoins in Electrum

After setting up Electrum, go to Wallet > Private Keys > Import, and type in the private key on your tip sheet.
